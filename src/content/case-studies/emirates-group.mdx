Four years inside Emirates Group's revenue accounting system, via DXC
Technologies, the engineering foundation the rest of this career is built
on.

## The problem

Airline revenue accounting is unforgiving: interline sales between carriers,
tax computation across jurisdictions, proration of multi-leg fares, and
passenger and cargo processing, all of it has to reconcile, at the scale of
one of the world's largest airlines.

## What I built

- New components integrated into the existing revenue accounting system via
  **REST and SOAP APIs**, working closely with business teams to nail down
  requirements before writing code.
- **Builds and deployments through Jenkins** as part of the team's CI/CD
  setup.
- **Structured logging and production support**, debugging and resolving
  issues without much escalation.

The environment set the standard I still work to: clean code, rigorous code
reviews, and structured testing.

## Stack

React, React Native, Redux, Node.js, SQL, REST, SOAP, Big Data (Hadoop),
Jenkins, Cypress.

## Impact

Multiple **Najm Awards** for delivery quality, **2nd place in the Emirates
Big Data Hackathon** (Hadoop, 2017), and a production-support record of
resolving issues at the source rather than escalating them.
