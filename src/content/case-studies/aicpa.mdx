The AICPA Spanish Hub (aicpa.org) brought the American Institute of CPAs'
member platform to Spanish-speaking accountants, delivered at Accenture as
my first formal team-lead role.

## The problem

A bilingual member platform for a major professional body, delivered by a
5+ developer team inside a SAFe Agile program with fixed cadences, so quality
had to stay consistent across the codebase while the client got predictable
progress.

## What I built

- **Led the team day to day**: sprint planning, estimates, managing
  deliverables, and keeping the client updated within the SAFe delivery
  cadence.
- **Code and performance reviews** as a routine, coaching junior developers
  toward cleaner, more testable code.
- **Salesforce and AWS integrations** connected into the platform through
  API integrations.

## Stack

React Native, Redux, Node.js, GraphQL, AWS, Salesforce, Cypress, Puppeteer.

## Impact

Consistent quality across a multi-developer codebase and a delivery rhythm
the client could plan around, proof that the engineering standards from
Emirates transfer to leading a team, not just writing the code.
